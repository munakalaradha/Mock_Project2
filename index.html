About Us

Founded in 2019 and officially incorporated in 2023, Thinkvate Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a government-recognized MSME (UDYAM-AP-10-0075552) led by seasoned professionals in control and instrumentation. In 2024, we initiated our flagship digital transformation journey, and by 2025, strategically evolved into a digital-first AI technology company. Thinkvate specializes in developing AI-driven SaaS platforms designed to optimize industrial operations, improve vendor lifecycle management, and enhance decision-making through data intelligence. Our solutions address real-world challenges across India’s mid-sized and MSME manufacturing landscape. With a mission to accelerate industrial digitization, we blend deep domain expertise with smart automation—delivering measurable outcomes, operational resilience, and a competitive edge for modern enterprises in an increasingly connected economy.
